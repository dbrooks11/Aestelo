# Aestelo

**Location-based social discovery for fashion photography**

Aestelo is a mobile platform that connects fashion enthusiasts with the perfect photogenic locations for their content. Scroll through a curated feed of aesthetic spots, explore locations on an interactive map, and document your visits with photos and music. Every location is specifically curated for streetwear photography and fashion content creation.

## What is Aestelo?

Finding the right area for your fashion photography shouldn't require endless searching through generic location apps or scrolling through hundreds of social media posts. Aestelo solves this by creating a dedicated platform where users can:

- Browse a curated feed of photogenic locations optimized for fashion and streetwear photography
- Explore spots on an interactive map to find locations near you
- View real outfit photos taken at each location to see exactly what works
- (Coming soon: Document your visits with photos and soundtrack them with music)
- Save favorite spots and build personal location collections
- (Coming soon: Connect with other fashion creators through Circles—curated groups that explore locations together)
- Share authentic recommendations within the fashion photography community

## The Vision

Aestelo aims to bridge the gap between location discovery and fashion content creation. Rather than adapting general-purpose mapping tools or wading through unfiltered social media, creators can access a platform purpose-built for their specific needs.

By combining location intelligence with social features, Aestelo creates a community where discovering your next perfect shoot location is as intuitive as finding your next creative collaborator.

## Target Audience

- Fashion photographers and content creators
- Streetwear enthusiasts building their personal brand
- Fashion influencers seeking fresh, authentic locations
- Style-focused individuals who value aesthetic curation
